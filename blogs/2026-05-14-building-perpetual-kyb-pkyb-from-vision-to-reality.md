Alloy developed a perpetual KYB (pKYB) solution addressing the need for continuous business verification beyond one-time onboarding checks. The article details how prior approaches fell short and explains the architecture Alloy built to detect registry changes, verify updated entities, recalculate risk across entire business groups, and escalate to human reviewers only when warranted.
